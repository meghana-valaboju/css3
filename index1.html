Tollywood refers to the Telugu language film industry based in Hyderabad, India. It is also known as the Telugu cinema. It's one of the largest film industries in India, known for producing a high volume of films annually.

PAWAN KALYAN

Artist & Politician

He is among the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema and has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list multiple times since 2012.

Artist

Artist
